    Caffeines role in blocking sleep

    Brandon Cole 25 Oct 2023 on Sleep, Health, Caffeine


    Notes:

    Caffeine acts to block the adenosine receptors that contribute to the build up of sleep pressure. Caffeine also has a half life of 5 to 7 hours. This means that as we continue to drink caffeine throughout the day we have to wait a certain amount of time for the adenosine receptors to be empty of caffeine.

    Source:

    PhD, M. W. (2017). Why We Sleep: Unlocking the Power of Sleep and Dreams (Illustrated edition). Scribner.
