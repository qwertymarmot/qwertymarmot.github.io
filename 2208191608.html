    How social circles impact the paths of life

    Brandon Cole 23 Oct 2023 on Decisionmaking, Success


    Notes:

    Our lives tend to take a the path of least resistence. Certain factors in our lives can heavily influence the likely path our lives will take. These factors include;

    • The family we are born into
    • The high school we attend
    • The college we attend
    • Our first job
    • Marriage For example if we go to a college where most of the graduates end up in the finance industry it is very likely we will also be influenced to pursue a career in the finance industry. These paths help to explain the network effects that allow the web if influence in the upper class to allow their immediate familes to continue down the path of upper class status and receive the preferential treatment and opportunties that apply to that group.

    Source:

    Your Life is Driven by Network Effects. (2020, February 25). NFX. https://www.nfx.com/post/your-life-network-effects/
