    Welcome to my Personal Index

    This index is a collection of the areas that are important to my personal growth and development. 11 Collect acts as a Inbox or Fleeting notes section for me to gather information that may not be permanent. 12 Active Thoughts is a workbench for things that are top of mind in existing or new knowledge areas. 13 Goals is simply a listing of my personal and professional goals. 14 Ideas is a collection of ideas that have made it past the collect box and but have not evolved into project yet. 15 Projects is the area for active projects that I am working on. Lastly below you will see a copy of my 12 Favorite Problems. I developed this list in Tiago Forte’s Buiding a Second Brain course.

    My 12 Favorite Problems

    • How can I become more efficient in idea retention?
    • How can I use emotional intelligence to excel as a leader?
    • What can I do to make sure that I have a positive attitude everyday?
    • What can be done to negate misunderstanding?
    • How can I translate complex ideas to someone with little to no technical understanding of those ideas?
    • How do I structure my day for optimal performance and habit building?
    • How do my interests translate into a successful career?
    • How can I share my thoughts and ideas to help others?
    • What needs to be done with money now to help me in the future?
    • How can I create meaningful connections to people who help me grow as a person?
    • How do I identify people who can help me grow?
