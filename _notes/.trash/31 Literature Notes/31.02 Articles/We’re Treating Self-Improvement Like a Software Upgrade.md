## Highlights
- All of our abilities can be improved upon and all of our parts are replaceable. Upgradable.
- Health, happiness, and humanity itself are all reducible to data points and subject to optimization.
- We—or the companies selling the improvements—are actively picking which features of humanity to enhance and which to suppress or ignore
- The human traits that are not favored by the market will surely be abandoned.
