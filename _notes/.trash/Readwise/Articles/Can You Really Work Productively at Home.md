## Highlights
- In the workplace, you had some distractions in the form of water cooler chats and lunchtime breaks, but these never held the allure of unsupervised time online shopping, videogaming, or just plain napping.
- The findings suggested that the drop in productivity associated with their new-found status amounted to about 1%; surprisingly, people with children showed less of a drop (2%) than those with no one but themselves to care for (a 3% drop).
